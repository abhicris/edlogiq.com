About Us

The Basics

EDLogIQ stands for Education Logic and IQ.We are a company focused in Brain Fitness Sector developing innovative products for enhancing human brain health. We have been working in this domain since 2012.

Our Culture

Everyone at EDLOGIQ thrives on challenge and the hope of making a difference in people's lives. Immersed in an environment of continuous learning, we at EDLOGIQ work each day to build and test new ideas, to share feedback and knowledge. Our team engages in a dynamic, exploratory process of bringing groundbreaking ideas to market.

Careers @ EDLogIQ

Work With us.

EDLOGIQ provides full time employment as well as internships to motivated and creative individuals who are looking for the ‘next big thing’. Small teams and a blazing pace mean that your contributions will matter.

The problems you tackle this week may be completely different from the ones you solved last week—but whatever the task, you'll be challenged to improve EDLOGIQ in new and exciting ways. Working with us gets you a seat in one the fastest moving vehicles of growth.
